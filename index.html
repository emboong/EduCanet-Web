Welcome to EduCaNet


a Learning Management System (LMS) specifically for Special Education (SPED) schools to facilitate access to informative resources and allow monitoring of student progress.

About EduCaNet


The program, extensively tested by SPED teachers and parents in Davao City, Philippines, caters specifically to children with special needs. It offers customized educational materials and resources, accessible even in remote areas with internet connectivity. Notably, it includes a feature for tracking and monitoring student progress, ensuring personalized and effective learning for every child, regardless of location.
